About Us

Welcome to Khan and Sons, your premier destination for top-notch groceries in Sangareddy. As a trusted and established Kirana shop, we take immense pride in providing a meticulously curated selection of premium quality grocery items at prices that won't stretch your budget. With a commitment to excellence that goes beyond our products, we offer the added convenience of free home delivery, ensuring that your culinary needs are met with utmost ease.

At Khan and Sons, we understand the importance of a diverse and high-quality grocery range, and our shelves are stocked with only the finest products. Whether you're stocking up on monthly rations, planning for a hotel event, or organizing a wedding function, Khan and Sons is your reliable partner in delivering quality, affordability, and convenience. We gladly accept bulk orders, ensuring that you not only receive the best value for your money but also experience the unmatched quality of our products without compromise.

Situated in the heart of Sangareddy, Khan and Sons invites you to experience a perfect blend of quality groceries, affordable prices, and the convenience of free home delivery. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we look forward to serving you with a warm smile as your trusted Kirana shop.
